Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Halloween AI ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and enjoyable experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Halloween AI.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos and Images

When you use Halloween AI, you may upload photos from your device's camera roll or take new photos using your device's camera. These photos are processed by our AI system to generate Halloween-themed transformations.

2.2 Generated Images

The Halloween-themed images created by our AI are stored locally on your device. We do not upload these images to our servers or any third-party services.

2.3 Anonymous Usage Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app. This may include:

  • App features you use
  • Time spent in the app
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crashes and performance data

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Image Processing

Your photos are processed through Google's Gemini AI service to generate Halloween-themed transformations. During processing, images are temporarily transmitted to Google's servers via a secure, encrypted connection. Once the transformation is complete, images are not retained by Google or our services.

3.2 Local Storage

All generated images are stored locally on your device in the app's secure storage directory. You have full control over these images and can delete them at any time through the app's history feature.

3.3 App Improvement

We use anonymous analytics data to:

  • Understand how users interact with our app
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Develop new features based on user preferences

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI service for image generation. When you create a Halloween transformation, your photo is temporarily sent to Google's servers for processing. Google's privacy policy and data handling practices apply to this service. You can review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Firebase Services

We use Firebase (a Google service) for:

  • Anonymous user authentication
  • Anonymous analytics
  • App security (Firebase App Check)

Firebase's privacy policy can be found at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.3 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or photos to any third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your photos and generated images are stored locally on your device. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect data stored in the app.

5.2 Data Transmission

When images are sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing, they are transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

5.3 Data Retention

Your photos and generated images remain on your device until you manually delete them through the app's history feature or by uninstalling the app.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have complete control over your images stored in the app. You can:

  • View all your generated images in the history section
  • Delete individual images at any time
  • Share images to social media or save them to your device's camera roll

6.2 Data Deletion

To delete all your data from the app:

  • Delete individual images through the history screen
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

6.3 Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable analytics through your device's settings by limiting ad tracking or disabling analytics in your iOS privacy settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Halloween AI is suitable for users of all ages. However, we recommend parental supervision for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notice in the app
  • Sending you a notification (if you have opted in)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. International Data Transfers

Our services use Google Cloud services, which may process data in various locations around the world. By using Halloween AI, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You provide consent when you upload photos for transformation
  • Legitimate Interests: We process anonymous analytics to improve our services
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to provide the app's core functionality

15. Your Responsibilities

When using Halloween AI, you are responsible for:

  • Only uploading photos you have the right to use
  • Respecting others' privacy when sharing transformed images
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations when using the app
  • Keeping your device secure to protect your stored images
